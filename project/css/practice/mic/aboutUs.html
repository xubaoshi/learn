Company Profile

Focus Imp. & Exp. Services Co., Ltd(referred as “FIE”) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Focus Technology Co., Limited, a Nanjing-headquartered public company (Stock Code: 002315) established in1996. FIE is committed to providing effective cost-saved export process service and financing service to global buyers and Chinese suppliers.

Focus Technology Co., Limited is an awarded multinational group whose core businesses include Business-to-Business (B2B) e-commerce, global trading service, education, recruitment, insurance agency, IT and logistics. The best known property of the group is Made-in-China.com (MIC), which is considered as one of the best e-commerce platform in B2B industry of China.

MIC and Us

Made-in-china.com is a well-known Business-to-Business (B2B) platform promoting business between Chinese suppliers and global buyers. Focus Imp. & Exp. Services Co., Ltd (referred as “FIE”)is a one-stop trade service provider which aims to help Chinese small-medium manufacturers save export process cost and conclude deals with buyers through financing method.

A large number of Audited suppliers located on Made-in-china.com have signed up for FIE’s service.
