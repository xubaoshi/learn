Imp Exp Service Financing Service(iCredit)

iCredit is a loan program to help small-to-medium sized businesses ("Buyers") purchase products from Chinese Suppliers ("Suppliers"). It enables Buyers to purchase products and delay payment for up to 90 days. Through iCredit, Buyers can receive shipments of goods first and pay later using short-term financing up to $750,000.

iCredit Features Introduced

  • Financing Up to USD $750,000
  • Delay Payment for Up to 90 Days
  • Easily Access Audited Suppliers
  • One-Stop Global Trade Service
